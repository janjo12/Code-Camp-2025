The game has 4 phases, you choose how many rounds within each phase.

Take turns rolling a die in order. Tap the number for each roll to add the face value to the rounds total.

Press stop anytime to add the round total to your score.

If anyone rolls a 4, the round ends and all players still rolling get 0.

Each phase will add a new rule:

Phase 2 total score + 50

Phase 3 total score + 100

Phase 4 total score x 2

After all 4 phases, the highest total wins!
